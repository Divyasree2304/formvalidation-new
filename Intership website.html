Summer internships allow college students to get real-world work experience. These programs help students build their resumes and take the first step toward a successful career after graduation. However, students aren’t the only ones who benefit from internship programs. Companies that offer these programs get a first look at new talent (i.e., the interns) before they enter the workforce. Internship programs also benefit a company’s culture and its bottom line. It’s the kind of mutually beneficial relationship that makes for good business.
